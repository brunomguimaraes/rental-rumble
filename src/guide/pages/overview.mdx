Rental Rumble battles resolve on their own: you draft the team and set the order,
then each fight plays out turn by turn with no inputs. These pages document
exactly what happens under the hood — the stats, the signs and the move logic —
so nothing about a result is a mystery.

Use the menu to jump around:

- **Runs** — draft, ladder shape, post-battle rewards, and difficulty.
- **The four stats** — what HP, Attack, Defense and Speed each do.
- **Zodiac signs** — the birth signs that tilt every Pokémon's stats.
- **Move pools** — what each Pokémon can throw, and why.
- **Status conditions** — burn, poison, paralysis, sleep, confusion.
- **Abilities** — the passives that bend the battle itself.
- **Type effectiveness** — an interactive chart of every matchup.
