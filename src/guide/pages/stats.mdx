# The six stats

Every Pokémon is described by six numbers. They are the foundation everything
else — signs, moves, status — pushes and pulls on.

<StatGrid />

Damage is **split into two channels**. Every move is either *Physical* or
*Energy* (or a no-damage *Status* move): a physical move pits the attacker's
**Physical Attack** against the defender's **Physical Defense**, while an energy
move pits **Energy Attack** against **Energy Defense**. So a wall that shrugs off
punches can still crumble to beams — bring the right kind of hit.

All fights run at **Level 50**, so these are the only stats that matter; there's
no experience, no levelling and no held items beyond the run-wide relics.
