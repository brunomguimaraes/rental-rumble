# Zodiac signs

Every Pokémon is born under one of twelve signs, which nudges its stats up or
down (shown below versus its base line). The twelve break down as four
**elements** — the broad archetype — each split into three **modalities** that
set the flavour: cardinal initiates (Speed-lean), fixed is stubborn/extreme,
mutable is rounded. A sign is an identity, so any Pokémon can carry any sign —
but the draft favours the ones that suit its stats.

## Celestial signs (rare)

Beyond the twelve, a Pokémon can rarely be born under a **celestial sign** — an
off-the-ecliptic constellation with a big, mixed stat boost and an animated
rainbow card frame. Four **rare** wanderers exist, plus the mythic **Abhijit**,
which lifts every stat by half and wears a shimmering golden border. Opponents
roll these at half a player's odds; beat a special trainer who fielded one and
you can recruit it.
